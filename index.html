Eligibility Verification Intelligence System

A healthcare operations portfolio project for detecting insurance eligibility risk before scheduling, authorization, claim submission, or patient billing are affected.

Built by Kori Pickle to demonstrate eligibility verification workflow analysis, patient access risk detection, clean intake readiness, and remote-ready healthcare operations documentation.

No PHI / Synthetic Data Notice

All data shown in this project is synthetic and created for portfolio demonstration purposes. No patient information, protected health information, or real payer case data is used.

Core Front-End Revenue Cycle Insight

Eligibility errors look small at intake, but they can trigger authorization delays, claim denials, patient billing confusion, staff rework, and revenue leakage downstream.

Recruiter Quick Read

A 60-second overview of who built this, what it solves, and what it proves.

Who built this?

Kori Pickle, Healthcare Administration student focused on healthcare operations, patient access, revenue cycle workflow analysis, and denial prevention.

What problem does it solve?

EVIS identifies eligibility risk before it disrupts scheduling, authorization, claim submission, or patient billing.

What skills does it prove?

Eligibility workflow analysis, clean intake readiness, KPI design, risk scoring, executive reporting, synthetic data design, and remote-ready documentation.

What roles does it support?

Patient Access Representative, Eligibility Verification Specialist, Prior Authorization Specialist, Revenue Cycle Analyst, and Healthcare Operations Analyst roles.
