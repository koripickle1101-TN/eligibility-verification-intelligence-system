Coverage Verification Checklist

A front-end checklist for reducing coverage confusion, payer mismatch, COB issues, referral uncertainty, and downstream denial risk.

Checklist

Verification GateQuestion
Coverage StatusIs coverage active for the expected date of service?
Payer MatchDoes the payer on file match the current coverage source?
Plan TypeIs the plan type clearly identified?
DemographicsDo patient name, DOB, member ID, and address match payer records?
COBIs coordination of benefits risk resolved?
ReferralIs a referral required before scheduling or service?
Authorization TriggerDoes the plan or service type trigger prior authorization review?
Patient ResponsibilityHas patient responsibility visibility been reviewed?

Executive Rule

Clean intake readiness starts with verified coverage, matched payer data, and visible patient responsibility risk.
