# LinkedIn Launch Post

I built EVIS 1.0 to explore a front-end revenue cycle question that matters before prior authorization, claim submission, or patient billing are affected:

What if eligibility errors are not small intake issues, but early warning signs of downstream workflow failure?

EVIS 1.0 is a healthcare operations portfolio project focused on insurance eligibility risk, coverage verification, payer mismatch, coordination of benefits, authorization triggers, patient responsibility visibility, clean intake readiness, and downstream denial prevention.

The project uses synthetic data only. No patient information, protected health information, or real payer case data is used.

This project demonstrates patient access workflow analysis, eligibility verification logic, KPI design, risk scoring, executive reporting, and remote-ready healthcare operations documentation.
